## Tips

- **Clip tightly** on the consonant/vowel sound, trim leading/trailing silence.
- **Multiple variants** are good: `AH0.wav`, `AH1.wav`, `AH2.wav` for different stress levels.
- **Format**: 44100 Hz mono WAV, PCM_16.
- **Test**: `python generate.py` then type a sentence to hear results.
- **Check**: `python -m ttsp.engine "hello world" -v kat` to test specific words.

## Stress Markers

- `0` = unstressed (AH0, EH0, IH0)
- `1` = primary stress (AH1, EH1, IH1)
- `2` = secondary stress (AH2, EH2, IH2)

The system strips stress markers automatically, so `AH.wav` works for all three.
